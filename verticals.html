NSDM vertical research

One boundary architecture, tested across real decision environments.

Each vertical changes the evidence, rules, consequences, user rights, and appropriate action. NSDM keeps one core architecture while creating domain-specific profiles.

Telecom

Network, customer, billing, and agent decisions

Vendor AI claims, service assurance, fraud, billing, network autonomy, data sovereignty, and human override.

QSR and retail

Choice, pricing, promotion, and service recovery

Decision friction, impulse, conflict, menu architecture, recommendation justification, trust, and abandonment.

Healthcare

Triage, evidence, and escalation

Source quality, contraindications, patient context, clinical authority, and emergency-routing thresholds.

Finance and credit

Underwriting, fairness, and appeal

Document evidence, adverse decisions, bias exposure, auditability, and cost per justified decision.

Cybersecurity

Refusal, assistance boundaries, and incident response

Dual-use code, malicious intent, safe transformations, SOC escalation, and adversarial testing.

Product assurance

Does the product actually work?

Authentication, uploads, reports, checkout, exports, flow evidence, and release gates.

Public sector

Authority, rights, and contestability

Eligibility, prioritisation, benefits, enforcement, appeal, and procurement claims.

NeuroBench

Decision health and multimodal evidence

EEG, gaze, EDA, heart-rate, facial-action evidence, task telemetry, and ethical intervention limits.

Enterprise AI and TokenOps

Model routing, cost, and justification

Cost per justified decision, context waste, vendor dependence, data exposure, and routing governance.
