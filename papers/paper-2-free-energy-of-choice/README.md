# Paper 2 — The Free Energy of Choice

Status: working theory paper.

This paper develops the NSDM-F architecture and the Variational Decision Hypothesis. It argues that world models, neuro-symbolic reasoning, causal representation learning, active inference, and drift-diffusion decision theory can be interpreted as converging on a shared variational decision framework.
