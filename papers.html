Working-paper programme

The NSDM paper roadmap.

Working papers are active manuscripts. Planned papers have defined research questions and structures but are not yet complete or peer-reviewed.

Working paper 01

Decision Boundaries and Benchmark

Core NSDM theory, evidence/governance/action labels, benchmark construction, and evaluation metrics.

Outputs: theory manuscript, GOV-0 benchmark, evidence-object schema.
Working paper 02

Neurosymbolic Decision Systems

Neural perception, symbolic representation, provenance, constraint checking, and governance-aware action selection.

Outputs: reference architecture, test cases, failure taxonomy.
Planned paper 03

Free Energy of Choice / NSDM-F

Decision effort, uncertainty reduction, and action selection through a falsifiable free-energy-inspired frame.

Planned paper 04

Entropy of Choice / NSDM-E

Option complexity, information overload, and the boundary between useful support and unjustified intervention.

Planned paper 05

Mathematical Foundations and Gödel Boundaries

Candidate generation, verification, proof, provenance, and under-specification in mathematical tasks.

Planned paper 06

Sovereign Stack Boundaries

Vendor dependence, data exposure, model routing, local control, decision authority, and human override.

Planned paper 07

Benevolent System Boundaries

Whom an AI decision serves, which values it advances, and what it sacrifices.

Planned paper 08

Neuroinference and NSDM NeuroBench

Sensor evidence, probabilistic cognitive markers, decision pathways, and bounded interventions.

Publication policy: No manuscript is presented as peer-reviewed or validated before it is. Public papers will include method disclosure, source trail, limitations, experiment status, and AI-assistance disclosure.
